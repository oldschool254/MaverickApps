What is the maximum allowable load for a 3/4-inch Grade 70 transport chain used in rigging?

AI ASSISTANT

A 3/4-inch Grade 70 transport chain has a working load limit of 4,700 lbs in a straight pull. In a basket hitch configuration that increases to 9,400 lbs, but in a choker hitch it drops to approximately 3,525 lbs. Always verify the manufacturer's rating stamped on the chain before rigging.

FOREMAN

What is the required trench depth before shoring or sloping is mandatory on a job site?

AI ASSISTANT

Per OSHA Standard 29 CFR 1926.652, any trench 5 feet deep or more requires a protective system such as shoring, sloping, or a trench box — unless the excavation is in stable rock. For trenches 20 feet or deeper, a registered professional engineer must design the system.
