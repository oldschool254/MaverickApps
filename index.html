About Us
THE ONE STAR LABOR STORY.

One Star Labor Solutions was born from a simple but powerful idea: every organization deserves access to professional technology — not just the ones with $50,000 budgets.

Founded by Ronny Hatfield in Freeport, Texas, One Star Labor Solutions started with a simple observation: people in the field spend too much time hunting for documents they should have instant access to. Whether it's a safety procedure, a company policy, or a rate schedule — when you need it, you need it now. Ronny decided to build the solution himself.

He set out to change that. Using cutting-edge AI technology, One Star Labor Solutions transforms any document — union contracts, employee handbooks, restaurant policies, government procedures, safety manuals — into a beautiful, searchable mobile app that lives right in your pocket.

What used to cost tens of thousands of dollars and months of development, One Star Labor Solutions delivers in days for a fraction of the price. That's the One Star Labor way — independent, bold, and built different.
