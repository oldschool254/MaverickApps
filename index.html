About Us
THE MAVERICK STORY.

Maverick Apps was born from a simple but powerful idea: every organization deserves access to professional technology — not just the ones with $50,000 budgets.

Founded by Ronny Hatfield in Freeport, Texas, Maverick Apps was built from real-world experience. As a union member, Ronny saw firsthand how difficult it was for workers to quickly access their contracts in the field. Important rights, wage rates, and grievance procedures buried in binders and PDFs that nobody could find when they needed them most.

He set out to change that. Using cutting-edge AI technology, Maverick Apps transforms any document — union contracts, employee handbooks, restaurant policies, government procedures, safety manuals — into a beautiful, searchable mobile app that lives right in your pocket.

What used to cost tens of thousands of dollars and months of development, Maverick Apps delivers in days for a fraction of the price. That's the Maverick way — independent, bold, and built different.
